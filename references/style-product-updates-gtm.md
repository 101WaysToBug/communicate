# Communication Style: Product Updates GTM

## Purpose
A go-to-market product update for announcing new features or changes to customers and internal stakeholders (marketing, sales, CS). Bridges the gap between what was built and how it should be positioned, messaged, and rolled out.

## Format Rules
- **Length:** 300-600 words
- **Tone:** Clear, customer-centric, enabling — written so sales, marketing, and CS teams can immediately use it
- **Structure — use these sections:**
  1. **Feature Name** — Clear, customer-facing name
  2. **Value Proposition** — One-liner on why this matters to customers
  3. **Target Audience** — Who benefits and which segments to prioritize
  4. **Key Messaging** — 3-4 bullet points capturing the core narrative for marketing, sales, and CS teams
  5. **How It Works** — Brief functional overview for enablement (not technical implementation)
  6. **Rollout Plan** — Availability (plans, regions, timeline) and any prerequisites
  7. **Supporting Assets Needed** — List of collateral to produce (help docs, demo video, blog post, in-app tooltip, etc.)
- **Include:** Customer-facing language, segment targeting, clear messaging pillars
- **Avoid:** Engineering details, internal acronyms, vague timelines, feature-only descriptions without value framing

## Example

**Input:** We're launching CRM call engagement logging for Zoho CRM and HubSpot — call activity including recording URL, transcription, and summarization gets automatically logged to the CRM. Available for Pro and Business plans.

**Output:**

## Feature Name
**CRM Call Engagement Logging — Zoho CRM & HubSpot**

## Value Proposition
Every WhatsApp call automatically logged in your CRM with recording, transcription, and summary — so your team never loses context and managers get full visibility without manual data entry.

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Sales and support teams using Zoho CRM or HubSpot alongside Wati
- **Secondary:** Operations and RevOps teams who need call data for reporting and compliance
- **Segments to prioritize:** Mid-market and enterprise accounts with existing CRM workflows

## Key Messaging
- **Zero manual logging** — Call activity flows into your CRM automatically, eliminating data entry and ensuring nothing falls through the cracks
- **Full context in one place** — Recording URL, AI transcription, and call summary appear directly on the CRM contact record
- **Manager visibility** — Sales leaders can review call quality and outcomes without switching tools
- **Works with your existing workflow** — Plugs into your current Zoho CRM or HubSpot setup with no migration required

## How It Works
- When a WhatsApp call ends, Wati automatically creates an engagement/activity record in the connected CRM
- The record includes: call duration, recording URL, AI-generated transcription, and call summary
- Logged under the matching contact record — agents and managers can view it directly in the CRM timeline
- Configurable per number and CRM instance from Wati settings

## Rollout Plan
- **Available on:** Pro and Business plans
- **CRMs supported:** Zoho CRM, HubSpot (initial launch)
- **Prerequisite:** WhatsApp Calling enabled + active CRM integration
- **Timeline:** Rolling out week of [date]
